Help

This is an application to the practice of English speech via Google Speech Recognition API:

  • Push "Record"-button and hold it while you read aloud a phrase on the screen.
  • The voice will be recognized via Web Speech API and you will see differences between required and recognized phrases.
  • Listen your voice by click on "Listen"-button to detect defects in pronunciation.

The phrases are grouped in text: one line is one phrase.
You can choose a predefined text or create your own by click on a left icon in a header on the main screen.

Keep in mind
Recognition does not always work well.
Some phrases can be recognized incorrectly even with perfect pronunciation.

Recommendation
Use a high-quality microphone to improve recognition.

If you have any questions, comments or suggestions, just let me know lb.im@yandex.ru or visit project page.
